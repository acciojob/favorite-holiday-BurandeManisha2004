The Ramoji Film City Of Hydrabad


Ramoji Film City is a vast integrated film studio complex and tourist destination located near Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Spread over thousands of acres, it serves as both a production hub for Indian cinema and a themed attraction for visitors, making it one of the world’s largest film studio complexes.

  1. Film Studio Tours – Explore real movie sets and learn about filmmaking.
  2. Live Shows & Entertainment – Enjoy stunt shows, dance performances, and more.
  3. Adventure Park– Experience thrilling rides and fun activities.
  4. Beautiful Gardens – Relax in scenic spots like Japanese Garden and Mughal Garden.
  5. Eureka Theme Park – Discover historical and cultural attractions in a fun way.
    • Hyderabadi Biryani – A world-famous flavorful rice dish with spices and meat.
    • Haleem – A rich and creamy dish made with wheat, meat, and lentils.
    • Irani Chai – A popular tea with a unique taste enjoyed with biscuits.
    • Double Ka Meetha – A sweet dessert made from bread, milk, and dry fruits.
    • Osmania Biscuits – Famous tea-time snacks with a slightly salty taste.
